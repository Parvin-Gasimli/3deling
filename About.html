OUR TEAM

Our rich offer and positive relations with clients the company owes to the whole 3Deling team – professionals who work with passion. The crew consists of nearly 40 highly specialized engineers who got degrees on the best technical universities in Poland and abroad.

In the office in Kraków, we operate in sections such as 2D, 3D, BIM, PDMS, data preparation, IT, graphic design and surveying. Our head office is located in Poland, but we cooperate with clients from the whole world. What is more, we have our representatives in the United Kingdom, Norway and in the Middle East.

We are extremely proud of our innovation and IT department, thanks to which modern technologies and innovative solutions become elements of our basic offer. The software created by our IT specialists regularly helps in the process of data optimization.

We believe that every day is a good opportunity to expand your own knowledge, develop skills and gain experience. We improve qualifications through internal and external training, striving to make every employee grow according to their interests. Creating a friendly atmosphere is key to efficient and effective work and reflects the success of our projects.

For 10 years of existing our company, we have been expanding our team and offer. The most important factors that guide us in the process of recruiting new people are knowledge, creativity and professional integrity. The extensive knowledge of our employees and a well-integrated team are today the company’s largest asset.
